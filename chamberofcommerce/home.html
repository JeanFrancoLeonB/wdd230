Presidente Message

Good evening with everyone present. It is a great honor for me to address you for the first time as president of the Libertad Chamber of Commerce, a position that I assume today thanks to the trust that you have placed in each of the members that make up this new Board of Directors, with whom for several For months we have already been working on projects that in the short and long term do not offer to provide more and better services according to the new reality that we have to face, unthinkable challenges that have changed the lives of each of us, surely our families and our jobs and companies.
As you know, he accompanied Dr. Hermes Escalante as first vice president in this Board of Directors that has just concluded its 2020 term for 4 years. An important stage for me in this institutional training that has allowed to know specific issues of great importance, cross-cutting problems that affect society and our institution. In this sense, it has allowed us to identify all the threats that have been added to this great utility.
The pandemic, the health crisis are no longer our only problem. We face problems of another kind as well, problems of an economic nature. We have seen institutions that also enter into crisis. Our chamber of commerce has been able to stand firm in the face of these threats. The institutional trajectory of more than 118 years obliges and commits that its behavior must be impartial.
Likewise, it commits entrepreneurs, it commits small, medium and large entrepreneurs that also make up our institution, to continue working for the good of our city, our region and our country. The creation of jobs is one of the most important values ​​that must be considered within the tasks or business role.
