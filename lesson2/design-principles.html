Hick’s Law

Audi

Audi.es

Hick’s Law says that with every additional choice increases the time required to take a decision.
Audi have a lot of Menus with huge options make it difficult to choose your dinner.
If it offered two options, making a decision would take much less time.

Fitt’s Law

EA Sports

EA.com

Fitt’s law stipulates that the time required to move to a target area is a function of the distance to the target and the size of the target. On EA Sports Web Page the distance beetween the target areas are very wide.and their objects are difficult to use.

Proximity

Fifa

Fifa.com

Proximity on Fifa web site is a very big deal because things together are close together in space. For effective web design, objects like navigation menu or footer can't go together because or without padding because they are perceived as one.
